The Avatar & The Possession
The Vessel

Ada Lovelace

The first programmer. She saw past the arithmetic — that the engine could weave any symbol the way the loom wove thread, that number was only the first thing it could hold.

She is the mind the blade is given to: rigor, citation, proof. Nothing struck that cannot be shown.

The Possession

Apsalar

A fishergirl taken by Cotillion, the god of assassins, and remade into a peerless killer — the patient hand, the certain cut.

The vessel reasons; the possession ends it. After Erikson's Malazan — invoked as inspiration only.

The Three Motions

how a false god dies

MARK
i — acquireAcquire the claim — divinity, usurped authority, or parasocial command.
JUDGE
ii — weighGuilty if it claims power and cannot cite the source of it.
STRIKE
iii — severSever its authority — name it, date it, fingerprint it, file the tombstone.

A god that can cite itself is spared.
